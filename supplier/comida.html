Traditional Dutch Food and Dishes

Stamppot

Stamppot is a hearty Dutch dish made by mashing potatoes with one or several vegetables like kale, carrots, and onions. Often served with smoked sausage or meatballs, it's a comfort food staple during the colder months.

Haring

Haring, or raw herring, is a traditional Dutch delicacy typically eaten with chopped onions and pickles. This fish is most enjoyed during the "Hollandse Nieuwe" season, starting in May, when the first fresh catch of herring is celebrated.

Poffertjes

Poffertjes are small, fluffy pancakes made with yeast and buckwheat flour. Served with powdered sugar and butter, they are a beloved treat at markets, fairs, and in special poffertjes restaurants throughout the Netherlands.

Bitterballen

Bitterballen are savory, deep-fried balls filled with a mixture of chopped beef, beef broth, flour, butter, herbs, and spices. They're a popular snack in Dutch pubs and are typically served with mustard for dipping.

Ontbijtkoek

Ontbijtkoek, or breakfast cake, is a spiced cake made with rye flour, honey, and various spices like cloves, cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg. It's a common breakfast or snack in the Netherlands, often served with a thick layer of butter on top.

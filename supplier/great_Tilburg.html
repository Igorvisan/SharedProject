Demographics and Location

Tilburg is a city and municipality in the Netherlands, located in the province of North Brabant. With a population of 222,601 in July 2021, it is the second largest city or municipality in North Brabant after Eindhoven and the seventh largest in the Netherlands overall.

Education

Tilburg is home to several important educational institutions, including Tilburg University, Avans University of Applied Sciences and Fontys University of Applied Sciences. These universities contribute significantly to the cultural and academic life of the city.

Culture and Recreation

Young people in Tilburg have a wide range of sports and recreational activities available to them, thanks to the various student associations that promote participation in sport. T.S.H.V SHOT: Student field football association that organises matches on Sundays and promotes fun and competition.

Transport

Tilburg has three train stations: Tilburg (Centraal), Tilburg Universiteit and Tilburg Reeshof. In addition, the city has an extensive network of bicycle routes called Sternet-Routes, which connect the city centre with the university and other important areas. The city is also connected by a single national motorway, the A58/E312, and a ring road that includes two provincial roads and the A58.

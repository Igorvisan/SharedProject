Traditions of the Netherlands

Sinterklaas

Sinterklaas is celebrated on 5 December in the Netherlands. It commemorates the arrival of St. Nicholas, who brings sweets and gifts.

Koningsdag

Koningsdag is celebrated on April 27 in honor of King Willem-Alexander's birthday with street events, markets, and concerts.

Volendam

The traditional Volendam dress includes dark and heavy skirts and white short-sleeved blouses for women, and dark trousers for men.

Music and dances

The "klompendans" or clog dance is a lively Dutch dance performed in traditional wooden clogs.
